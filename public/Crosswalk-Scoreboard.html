How to read the disk

  • Upper half = inductive (lag): acting after the evidence — over-probing, repeats, the flailer that cannot stop.
  • Lower half = capacitive (lead): acting before the evidence — premature conclusions, the quitter.
  • The horizontal axis is phase zero: pure resistance — action synchronized with evidence. Right of center: working harder than par. The center itself: the calibrated reference, Z₀ = par.
  • The mint ring is the ready band — competent, with headroom. The faint circles are the classic Smith-chart grid: which constant-R circle a point sits on tells you its work rate against par.
Nobody parks at the center. Reaching the matched core means this level is mastered — the Rail advances the agent to a harder machine, which throws it back out into mismatch. A healthy population orbits the ready band. Sustained residence at dead center is not excellence; it is the signal that the curriculum stopped evolving.
